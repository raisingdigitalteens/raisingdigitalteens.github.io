Before you hand over
that phone —
did you know?

Most parents don't realize how much control is already built into their child's iPhone. No extra apps. No monthly fees. It's all there — you just have to turn it on.

Here's what you can actually control
📲
App Downloads
Can your child install any app they want?
By default — yes. Every app in the App Store is one tap away. But you can require your approval before anything installs, or block new downloads entirely.
✓ You can control this
🌐
Web Browsing & Content
What can they look up online?
By default — everything. Adult content, unmoderated forums, anything. You can filter explicit content, enable SafeSearch, or limit browsing to only sites you approve.
✓ You can control this
💬
Social Media
Should they have access to social media?
Most platforms require users to be 13+. You can block social apps entirely, limit access to one approved platform, or set daily time limits with safety settings in place.
✓ You can control this
⏱️
Daily Screen Time
How long should they be on their phone each day?
You can set separate daily limits for social apps, entertainment, and games. When the limit is reached, those apps stop working — automatically, without a fight.
✓ You can control this
🌙
Bedtime & Overnight Access
Is the phone accessible at 2am?
By default — yes. You can set a schedule so the phone locks itself every night at a time you choose. No willpower required from anyone. It just stops working.
✓ You can control this
📞
Who Can Contact Them
Can strangers call or message your child?
By default — yes. You can limit calls and messages to contacts only during the day, and family only overnight. Emergency services always go through.
✓ You can control this
📍
Location Awareness
Do you know where they are?
Built into every iPhone — you can see your child's device location from your own phone. You decide whether your child knows it's on and how you use it.
✓ You can control this
💳
In-App Purchases
Can they spend money without you knowing?
By default — yes. In-app purchases are on and can charge without a password. You can require your approval for every purchase or block spending entirely.
✓ You can control this
